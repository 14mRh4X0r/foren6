Foren6

Visualize your 6LoWPAN network. Foren6 uses sniffers to capture 6LoWPAN traffic and renders the network state it in a graphical user interface.

Detect routing problems. The Routing Protocol for 6LoWPAN Networks, RPL, is an emerging IETF standard. Foren6 captures all RPL-related information and is capable of identifiying abnormal behaviors.

On-site diagnosis. Foren6 captures live packets from deployed networks in a non-intrusive manner.

Android support. An Android port is under development, allowing to visualize 6LoWPAN networks on a tablet. It is ideal for walking around in a WSN.

Debug-oriented. Rewind the packet capture history, replay a previous packet trace and navigate through different overlays to pinpoint problems.

BSD license. Foren6 is distributed through a 3-clause BSD licence, ideal for integration in commercial systems without restriction.

Foren6 is a 6LoWPAN network sniffer and diagnosis tool.
